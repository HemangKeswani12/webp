THE VISION

PHILOSOPHY

Sustainable Energy & Space

I believe the energy crisis isn't just a resource problem—it's an engineering challenge waiting for a human-centric solution. My work focuses on the intersection of sustainable energy management, specifically harnessing the immense potential of nuclear power, and pioneering space-based energy production systems like Dyson swarms.


From the quiet corridors of research institutes to the high-pressure environment of international debates, I've learned that technology without empathy is hollow. Whether I'm redesigning laptop cooling systems to extend hardware lifespans or leading teams to design orbital settlements for 10,000 people, my goal remains the same: to build systems that integrate seamlessly into the fabric of our daily lives.

RESEARCH & INNOVATION

PHYSICS

CERN Research Proposal

Collaborating with the Physics Department at IISER Pune, I delved into the complexities of high-energy physics. Our proposal for CERN wasn't just about theoretical particles; it was an attempt to bridge the gap between fundamental physics and practical energy applications. The process involved rigorous networking, interdisciplinary coordination, and a deep dive into the mechanics of the universe.

AEROSPACE

ARSSDC Space Settlement

Runner-up among 150 schools at the Asian Regional Space Settlement Design Competition. We weren't just building a model; we were designing a society. Balancing the cold constraints of aerospace engineering with human psychology, sociology, and economics, we engineered life support systems, gravity simulation, and social structures for a viable orbital habitat.

AUTOMOTIVE

F1 in Schools (Doppler)

Speed is a function of precision. As the Lead Engineer for Doppler Racing, I secured the National Best Engineer Award. We designed a miniature F1 car that covered 20 meters in just 1.144 seconds. This was the result of months of iterative design, CFD analysis, and wind tunnel testing—understanding airflow, reducing drag, and manufacturing with sub-millimeter tolerance.
